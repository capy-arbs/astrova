## Ship Firepower

Larger ships fire multiple projectiles per volley in a fan spread pattern. Each projectile deals full weapon damage.

| Ship Class | Projectiles | Examples |
|------------|-------------|---------|
| Small / Starter | 1 | Starter Ship |
| Medium (size 64) | 2 | Nairan Scout, Nairan Fighter, Smuggler, etc. |
| Battlecruiser | 3 | Battlecruiser |
| Super-Carrier | 3 | Super-Carrier |
| Battleship | 4 | Battleship |
| Dreadnought | 5 | Dreadnought |

Fan spread widens with more projectiles (0.08 radians per additional shot). Projectiles are offset slightly so they don't stack on top of each other.

## Drones

Carrier and Super-Carrier exclusive. Controlled with **V** key.

### Drone Management
- **V to deploy** — 5 second launch delay, drones enter combat
- **V to recall** — 5 second return, surviving drones go back to bay
- **Auto-replace** — lost drones replaced from bay every 3 seconds while deployed
- **Destructible** — drones have 8 HP, enemy bullets deal 1 damage each
- **Damage chance on contact** — 10% (Layer 1-2), 25% (Layer 2), 50% (Layer 3-4)
- **Restock at station** — 50cr per drone

### Drone Stats
| Ship | Deployed | Bay Max | Range |
|------|----------|---------|-------|
| Carrier | 4 (+skill) | 8 | 250px |
| Super-Carrier | 6 (+skill) | 16 | 350px |

### Drone AI
- Orbit carrier when no enemies nearby
- Engage nearest enemy within range (including police when wanted)
- Keep ~80px distance, strafe and shoot (500ms fire rate)
- Back off if too close, chase if too far
- Leash back to carrier if exceeding range

## Contraband & Smuggling

Buy at Haven (Alpha Centauri) and other frontier settlements. Sell at any station.

| Item | Buy Price | Sell Value |
|------|-----------|-----------|
| Stolen Tech | 40cr | 80cr |
| Black Market Arms | 60cr | 120cr |
| Smuggled Relics | 80cr | 150cr |
| Restricted Data | 100cr | 200cr |

### Contraband Sources
- **Haven (Alpha Centauri)** — All 4 types
- **Wreckage Field (Dead Zone)** — Stolen Tech
- **Exile (Outer Rim)** — Black Market Arms, Smuggled Relics
- **Anomaly Prime (Nebula)** — Restricted Data
- **Terminus (The Void)** — Restricted Data

### Police System
- Police patrol all Layer 1-2 systems (Sol: 4, Alpha Centauri: 3, Kepler: 2)
- **Scan range:** 80px (normal ships) / 25px (Smuggler) / 45px (Shadow Runner)
- **Scanning takes 2 seconds** — shows "SCANNING..." warning while in progress
- On detection: **contraband confiscated** + **fine charged** (25cr per item confiscated)
- Player becomes **WANTED** for 60 seconds
- Shooting police = **WANTED** for 90 seconds
- While wanted: police chase and shoot (blue bullets, 8 damage)
- Leaving scan range before 2 seconds resets the scan — use cloak timing for counterplay

### Cloaking (Smuggler ship only)
- Press **C** to toggle cloak
- Ship goes translucent — police can't scan or chase
- Drains 20 energy/sec (5 seconds of cloak)
- Recharges at 8/sec when off (~12.5s full recharge)
- Energy bar shown in HUD

Each planet has 12 resource nodes + chance for rare resources (hidden until scanned). Settlements have detailed building visuals matching their biome.

### Mining Mechanic
- **Hold to gather** — stand still near a resource node to mine it (was instant walk-over pickup)
- **Progress bar** appears showing mining progress
- **Must stand still** — moving cancels the gather
- **Base gather time:** 1 second
- **Mining skill reduces time:** down to 0.3 seconds at high levels (formula: 1.0 - level x 0.012)
- **Mining Charge buff:** +50% gather speed while active
- **Mining bonus:** +1 resource per 10 Mining levels + ship bonus (Prospector +1, Mining Barge +3)

### Planet Controls
| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|

| Shift | Sprint (1.6x speed) |
| Q | Scanner pulse (reveals resources + hidden rares) |
| I | Cargo screen |

## Mechanics

### Ship Physics
- **Speed** = base 150 + Piloting bonus + ship bonus
- **Acceleration** = varies by ship class (60-600)
- **Turn rate** = varies by ship class (0.012-0.18)
- **Drag** = 0.92 (ships slow when not accelerating)

### Docking
- Ship velocity zeroes and snaps to station position when docking
- No more drifting during station menus

### Shield Regen
- Starts after 3 seconds without taking damage
- Rate: 2/s + 0.1/s per Shields level
- Awards Shields XP while regenerating

### Hull Repair
- Starts after 6 seconds without taking damage
- Rate: 0.5/s + 0.08/s per Hull Integrity level
- Awards Hull Integrity XP while repairing

### Death
- Ship destroyed, cargo lost, reset to starter ship
- Skills: lose progress toward next level (keep current level)
- Keep: credits, discovered planets, completed missions
- Respawn in Sol

### Saving
- **S key** or cargo screen button for manual save
- **Auto-save** on system jumps
- **Reset** button in cargo screen (with confirmation)
- Uses localStorage

### Backgrounds
- Split-up layered starfield — clean parallax with no repeating artifacts
- No more condensed black holes or oversized star repetition
- Each system has a unique star tint:
  - Sol: default purple
  - Alpha Centauri: blue
  - Kepler: warm orange
  - Dead Zone: brown/dusty
  - Outer Rim: amber
  - Nebula: purple glow
  - Void: deep purple

### Station Sprite
- Detailed structure with solar panel arms
- Docking ring with rotating lights
- Windows and reactor core glow
- Replaced earlier plain rectangle stations

### Ship Upgrades
| Upgrade | Stat | Amount | Cost | Eng Level |
|---------|------|--------|------|-----------|
